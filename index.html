How does AuroraX work?

AuroraX harnesses the power of brainwaves to unlock new realms of cognitive potential and enhance mental performance.

Is EEG software safe to use?

Our EEG software is rigorously tested and designed with user safety as the top priority, ensuring a secure and reliable experience.

Can PEMFT improve focus?

Absolutely! PEMFT technology targets brain regions associated with focus and concentration, leading to enhanced cognitive abilities.

What is TACS used for?

Transcranial Alternating Current Stimulation (TACS) is utilized to modulate brain activity and explore its impact on cognitive functions.

Is TDCS suitable for everyone?

While generally safe, Transcranial Direct Current Stimulation (TDCS) should be used under professional guidance to ensure optimal results.
